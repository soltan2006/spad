In his own land, as in paradise

"SPADCHYNA - Belarus in Minecraft" is a unique project that immerses players in the virtual world of modern Belarus.

Advantages of the project

Uniqueness and creativity

The project offers an opportunity to plunge into the virtual embodiment of the cultural and historical heritage of Belarus, presented in the form of a gaming environment.

Educational context

Thanks to the project, players can learn more about Belarus, its culture, history and attractions, as well as get acquainted with the maps of our project, which you can download for your game.

Social value

The creation of the project contributes to the popularization of Belarus among players from around the world, which can attract attention to the cultural and tourist potential of the country, as well as contribute to the development of virtual tourism.
