About the project

SPADCHYNA is a grandiose project to create attractions of the Republic of Belarus in the style of the popular computer game Minecraft.

  1. Why is this project called "Spadchyna"?
  2. This project was named in honor of the 100th anniversary of the publication of Yanka Kupala's fourth collection “Spadchyna“ (1922). This is the first collection of Belarusian classics that was published for the first time in Minsk, the capital of young Belarus. The author of this work expresses great love for his native land, creating a number of vivid images and the main thing is the heritage, the Land that was left to us from our ancestors. The poet calls her the greatest treasure that Belarusians should preserve and preserve for posterity.

From that moment on, “SPADCHYNA” began to take shape as the first Internet project in Belarus to develop a virtual tour of our country in the style of the popular computer game Minecraft.

We invite you to an exciting journey through the virtual heritage, where every corner is saturated with the history and spirit of Belarus. Welcome to our unique world!
